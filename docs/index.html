Why This Exists

Japanese onomatopoeia
gets lost in translation.

When manga and anime are translated into English, the original Japanese sound words are often replaced — or just gone. But if you love Japanese pop culture, you know those sounds are the vibe.

A tiny desktop toy for anime fans, manga lovers, and anyone curious about Japanese sound words.

📖
In Japanese manga...
Sound effects are woven into the art itself. They're not just descriptions — they carry emotion, texture, and personality. Japanese has thousands of onomatopoeia that English simply doesn't have equivalents for.
ぽ (po) — a soft, gentle tap
ふわ (fuwa) — light, floating, airy
えへ (ehe) — a shy, embarrassed giggle
🌍
In English translation...
These nuanced sounds get flattened into generic English words — "tap", "whoosh", "heh" — or removed entirely. The charm of the original Japanese expression disappears.
ぐわ (guwa) → "CRASH!" (loses the guttural texture)
ぴょん (pyon) → "hop" (loses the bouncy lightness)
にゃ (nya) → "meow" (loses the cute anime nuance)

For Anime & Manga Fans

The sounds that get
lost in translation.

When you read the English translation of your favorite manga, the original Japanese sound effects are often replaced or removed. But those sounds — ぽ, ふわ, えへ — carry something that "tap", "whoosh", and "heh" just can't.

Japanese onomatopoeia is incredibly rich. There are words for the sound of silence, the feeling of something fluffy, the specific way a shy person laughs. This app lets you experience those sounds every time you type — not as a translation, but as the real thing.

Features

Everything you need.

⌨️
Global Key Detection
Works in any app — browser, code editor, terminal. Sounds play regardless of which window is focused.
🎵
Key-Type Sounds
Different sounds for letters, Space, Enter, Backspace, and modifier keys. Each category has multiple random variants.
✨
4 Sound Modes
Quiet, Fun, えへ, and Chaos. Switch instantly based on your mood or audience.
🔊
Volume Control
Fine-tune the volume to exactly where you want it. Night Mode automatically reduces volume to 50%.
⚡
Sound Throttle
Rapid keystrokes are automatically throttled so sounds never become overwhelming or annoying.
🌐
Bilingual UI
Auto-detects your OS language. Or manually switch between English and Japanese in Settings.
Get Started

Install in 3 steps.

1
Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/soramusubi-ctrl/onomatope-keyboard.git
cd onomatope-keyboard
2
Install dependencies
npm install
3
Launch the app
npm start
macOS note: On first launch, you'll need to grant Accessibility permission in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility. This is required for global keyboard detection.
